FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a tarot card reading course?+

It is a structured program that teaches you to interpret all 78 tarot cards and use spreads for guidance on love, career, relationships and self-growth.

Do I need prior experience?+

No. This course is beginner-friendly and starts with the basics before moving into practical readings.

Will I receive a certificate?+

Yes, you receive a Certificate of Completion after successfully completing the course.

Do I need to buy a tarot deck?+

A Rider-Waite style tarot deck is recommended because it is beginner-friendly and widely used for learning.

Can I offer readings after this course?+

Yes. By the end, you will understand card meanings, spreads, ethics, consultation flow and how to start a basic tarot reading practice.

Is tarot the same as fortune telling?+

Tarot is best approached as a tool for reflection, guidance and self-awareness rather than fixed prediction. The course teaches ethical framing.
