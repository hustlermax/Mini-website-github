Your Questions Answered

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your first session with Mini Govil.

What should I expect in my first session with Mini Govil?

+
Your first session is a calm, private conversation guided by your birth details. Mini will walk you through your Kundali or Tarot spread, explain key planetary influences, and answer your specific questions on career, relationships, or life direction. Sessions are available in Hindi and English, over phone or video call. Expect honesty, warmth, and actionable insight — not vague predictions.

Do I need to know my exact birth time for a Vedic reading?

+
An exact birth time gives the most precise Kundali reading, as it fixes the Lagna (rising sign) accurately. However, if you don't know it, Mini can still provide valuable insights using your birth date and place. She also uses Tarot and numerology to fill the gaps and deliver meaningful, grounded guidance.

How is Vedic astrology different from Western astrology?

+
Vedic astrology (Jyotish) uses the sidereal zodiac — based on the actual position of stars — while Western astrology uses the tropical zodiac based on the seasons. Vedic astrology also incorporates the Moon sign, rising sign (Lagna), Nakshatra constellations, and Dasha timing systems, making it exceptionally precise for life timing, karmic patterns, and spiritual insights. Your Vedic sun sign may differ from your Western one by roughly one zodiac sign.

Can astrology predict my future?

+
Astrology reveals the energies and patterns at play in your life — it highlights opportunities, likely timing, and potential challenges. Think of it as a cosmic GPS: it shows the road, but you hold the steering wheel. Mini's approach is always empowering, not fatalistic. She gives you the insight to make better, more conscious decisions.

How soon can I book a session?

+
Sessions can often be booked within 24–48 hours. Simply message Mini on WhatsApp (+91 74113 07621) with your preferred date, time, and the concern you'd like addressed. She is available Monday through Sunday, 11 AM–5 PM IST.

Are online / phone consultations as effective as in-person?

+
Absolutely. The accuracy of a Vedic or Tarot reading does not depend on physical proximity — it depends on your birth data and the reader's expertise. Thousands of Mini's clients across India and abroad have experienced deeply transformative readings over phone and video call. Distance is no barrier to the cosmos.

What is the difference between a tarot reading and astrology?

+
Astrology uses your birth chart to map long-term planetary cycles, karmic patterns, and life timing. Tarot uses a deck of 78 symbolic cards to offer intuitive, present-moment guidance — it is ideal for specific questions, decisions, or gaining clarity on an immediate situation. Mini often combines both modalities in a single session for a layered, deeply accurate reading.
